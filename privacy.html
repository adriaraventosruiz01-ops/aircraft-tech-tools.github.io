Privacy Policy

Aircraft Tech Tools • Last updated: 2026-02-18

Aircraft Tech Tools (“we”, “our”, “the app”) does not collect, store, or sell personal data.

Ads (Free Version)

The free version displays banner advertising. Ads may be provided by third-party partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may automatically collect certain information (for example: device identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, and diagnostic information) to deliver and measure ads, prevent fraud, and improve ad performance. We do not receive or store personally identifying information from these partners.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ads / Ad Technology: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Photos and Local Data

If you choose to capture a Tech Log photo inside the app, the photo is stored locally on your device (for example, in your gallery / local storage). We do not upload these photos to any server.

PDF Export and Sharing

The app can generate a PDF report that may include your entries and optional Tech Log photos. This report is created locally on your device. Sharing/exporting (WhatsApp, email, etc.) only happens if you explicitly choose to share it using your device’s share options.

Purchases (Pro Version)

If you purchase the Pro version, the purchase is processed by Apple App Store / Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment details. The store may provide the app with a non-personal entitlement status so the app can unlock Pro features.

Permissions

The app may request permission to:

  • Access photos/media (to save or attach Tech Log photos)
  • Storage access (to create/export the PDF)

You can manage permissions at any time in your device settings.
